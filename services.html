Our Services

Reflow Ovens

Wave Solder

  • Machine training for operators and engineers
  • Wave maintenance and optimization
  • Solder pot management and dross reduction
  • Process profiling for high-reliability builds

  • Operator and process training
  • Maintenance and nozzle setup

  • Profiling for mixed-technology assemblies

Stencils

  • Support for step-down and stepped stencils

Design for Manufacturability (DFM)

  • Panelization recommendations
  • Feedback for faster NPI and smoother transfer to production
